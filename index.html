EXTRAS & RESOURCES

Comprehensive Development Education Toolkit
An extensive collection of ready-to-use educational materials, practical assessment tools, and research resources supporting development education and practice across South Asian contexts. Each resource is designed for immediate implementation in classroom, workshop, and professional development settings.

Educational Handouts Library

35+ comprehensive workshop modules designed for 75-90 minute sessions, covering the full spectrum of development practice including public health, economics, data analysis, and social policy.

Featured Workshop Series:
  • Social Safety Nets: 2-part series with program design, targeting analysis, and policy evaluation
  • Public Health & Health Systems: WHO frameworks, equity analysis, intervention design, and CDC-developed curricula
  • Data Analysis Track: Data literacy, bivariate analysis, and visualization techniques
  • Development Economics: Growth theories, inequality measurement, and policy evaluation problem sets
  • Gender Studies: Quick reference guides, intersectionality frameworks, and practical applications
  • Education & Pedagogy: Learning theory, NEP 2020 implementation, and curriculum design
  • Livelihoods Assessment: Complete toolkit with frameworks, indicators, and measurement tools
Related Courses: All course modules have corresponding workshop handouts ready for classroom use

Assessment & Evaluation Frameworks

Professional-grade assessment frameworks and practical toolkits for program evaluation, organizational analysis, and impact measurement across health, livelihoods, and development sectors.

Assessment Toolkits:
  • Livelihood Assessment: Complete framework with indicators, data collection tools, and analysis methods
  • Public Health Assessment: Health system performance indicators, community health metrics, and equity analysis
  • Program Evaluation Templates: Logic models, theory of change frameworks, and impact measurement tools
  • Organizational Capacity: Systems analysis, capacity evaluation, and improvement planning tools
Related Courses: MEL 101, Livelihoods 101, Public Health 101, Research Ethics 101

Research Design Resources

Advanced research methodologies, design standards, and specialized analytical frameworks for rigorous development research and evidence generation.

Research Resources:
  • Research Design Standards: Comprehensive checklists for quantitative and qualitative research
  • Specialized Methods: "Understanding Attributable Risk" and advanced analytical techniques
  • Ethics Frameworks: Research ethics protocols, consent processes, and data protection guidelines
  • Data Collection Tools: Survey design, interview guides, and observation protocols
Related Courses: Research Ethics 101, Qualitative Research 101, Econometrics 101, MEL 101

Data Analysis Toolkit

Complete technical resource library including analysis scripts, Excel templates, Python code samples, and qualitative data analysis tools for development research and program evaluation.

Technical Resources:
  • Statistical Analysis: R and Python scripts for common development research analyses
  • Excel Templates: Ready-to-use templates for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization
  • Survey Analysis: Household survey analysis workflows and interpretation guides
  • Qualitative Tools: Coding frameworks, thematic analysis guides, and reporting templates
Related Courses: Data Literacy 101, EDA for Survey Data, Bivariate Analysis 101, Econometrics 101

Policy & Economics Resources

Advanced policy analysis tools, economic modeling frameworks, and practical resources for development economics research and policy evaluation in South Asian contexts.

Policy Analysis Tools:
  • Economic Modeling: Development economics problem sets, policy simulation models
  • Poverty Analysis: Measurement tools, inequality indices, and targeting evaluation frameworks
  • Policy Evaluation: Cost-benefit analysis templates, program impact assessment tools
  • Case Studies: South Asian policy experiences, comparative analysis frameworks
Related Courses: Development Economics 101, Poverty & Inequality 101, Social Safety Nets 101, Political Economy 101
