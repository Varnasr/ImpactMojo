Development Education for South Asia

About Dr. Varna Sri Raman

Development Economist, PhD

Dr. Varna Sri Raman is a development economist with extensive experience in South Asian contexts. Her work focuses on applied development economics, gender studies, and evidence-based policy making. She has worked with various international organizations and brings both academic rigor and practical experience to development education.
