ImpactMojo 101 Knowledge Series

ImpactMojo is an open-access library of full-length learning decks on climate, gender, health, justice, and data — created for researchers, facilitators, and social changemakers in India and South Asia.

Each of the 28 decks is carefully curated, regionally grounded, and designed to be directly usable in workshops, training, and self-guided learning.

  • Bivariate Analysis 101
    Statistical relationships between two variables — exploring associations through real-world examples.
  • Care Economy and Time Use 101
    Unpaid care work, gendered labor, and time-use surveys contextualized in Indian economic policy.
  • Caste Identity Exclusion 101
    Structural discrimination, caste hierarchies, reservation debates, and lived exclusion in South Asia.
  • Climate Resilience 101
    Vulnerability, adaptation, and climate risk in the Indian subcontinent — grounded in local examples.
  • Community Led Development 101
    Participatory tools, PLA, and models of grassroots governance and co-created planning.
  • Data Literacy 101
    Foundational literacy in reading, interpreting, and questioning data in development and public life.
  • Development Economics 101
    Growth, poverty, inequality, and policy — with Indian frameworks and South Asian grounding.
  • EDA 101
    Exploratory Data Analysis with real development datasets — visualizations, summaries, and distributions.
  • Econometrics 101
    Applied econometric reasoning — regressions, DiD, PSM — with policy examples from South Asia.
  • Education and Pedagogy 101
    Foundational learning, NEP 2020, pedagogical models, and inclusive education design.
  • Environmental Justice 101
    Rights, access, and procedural justice across air, water, and land — with an equity lens.
  • Fundraising 101
    Navigating the funding ecosystem — from grants to HNIs, CSR, and movement-led approaches.
  • Gender Studies 101
    Feminist theory, intersectionality, queer politics, and gender justice movements in South Asia.
  • Humanitarian vs Development 101
    Comparing relief, recovery, and development mandates in crises and protracted contexts.
  • Indian Constitution and Justice 101
    Fundamental rights, directive principles, PILs, and justice structures in India.
  • Instructional Design 101
    Designing learning for adults — scaffolding, outcomes, and learning pathways.
  • LDCs and Global Development 101
    International systems, WTO, UN, SDGs, and India’s role in global development governance.
  • Language and Linguistic Diversity 101
    Multilingualism, language policy, endangered dialects, and identity.
  • MEL 101
    Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning in development — logic models, adaptive systems, and outcomes.
  • Media BCC 101
    Behavior change communication, participatory media, and theory-grounded campaigns.
  • Nutrition Food Systems Culture 101
    Nutrition transitions, food sovereignty, cultural foodways, and public provisioning.
  • Political Economy 101
    Institutions, incentives, and political analysis for development practitioners.
  • Post Truth and Public Trust 101
    Disinformation, narrative power, and rebuilding trust in democratic institutions.
  • Public Health 101
    Health systems, equity, primary care, and public health frameworks contextualized in India.
  • Regression ANOVA Causal Inference 101
    Statistical reasoning, modeling, and inference using development research scenarios.
  • Social Research Ethics and Consent 101
    Consent, harm, positionality, and power in research processes.
  • Tribal Identities and Justice 101
    Adivasi worldviews, forest governance, autonomy, and cultural-political rights.
  • Visual Ethnography and Storytelling 101
    Photography, ethics, positionality, and field documentation through visual methods.
