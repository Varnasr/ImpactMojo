Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Browse our course library, choose a learning path that matches your interests, or start with foundational courses like Data Literacy 101 or Gender Studies 101.

Are the courses free?

Yes! All ImpactMojo 101 courses are freely available under Creative Commons licensing. Create an account to bookmark courses and track your progress.

Can I use these for teaching?

Absolutely! All content is licensed CC BY-NC-SA 4.0, meaning you can adapt and use materials for non-commercial educational purposes with attribution.

How often are new courses added?

We regularly add new courses and update existing content. Check our "Latest Additions" section or subscribe to updates to stay informed.

What devices do I need to access the courses?

All courses work on any device with internet access - computers, tablets, or smartphones. No special software required. Our platform is optimized for mobile learning, perfect for learners across South Asia with varying technology access.

Why focus specifically on South Asia?

Our courses use local case studies, policy examples, and cultural contexts from India and South Asia. This makes the learning more relevant and applicable than generic global content. However, the frameworks and concepts are valuable for development practitioners worldwide.

Important Disclaimer

The content provided in ImpactMojo courses is for educational purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, users should verify information independently and adapt content to their specific contexts. The courses represent academic perspectives and should not be considered as official policy guidance. We provide NO formal qualifications or certifications.
