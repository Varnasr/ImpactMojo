Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: At ImpactMojo, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password
  • Profile Information: Optional information you may add to your profile
  • Course Progress: Your course completion status, bookmarks, and notes
  • Communications: Information you provide when you contact us or subscribe to our newsletter

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on courses, features used
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring URLs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our educational services
  • To personalize your learning experience
  • To track your course progress and provide recommendations
  • To communicate with you about updates and new courses
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is stored using Firebase services with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments are conducted
  • We use secure HTTPS connections throughout our platform

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Firebase (Google) Authentication & Database Account data, course progress
Formspree Contact forms & Newsletter Email address, form submissions
GitHub Pages Website hosting None (static hosting)

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Remember your login status
  • Save your theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Store temporary course progress
  • Improve site performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your course notes and progress
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data is kept while your account is active
  • Course progress is retained to maintain your learning history
  • Deleted accounts are removed within 30 days
  • Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics

8. Children's Privacy

ImpactMojo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
