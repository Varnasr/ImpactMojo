Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please mention this in your subject line.

Can I request a new course topic?

Absolutely! We love hearing course suggestions from our community. Please use the contact form above and select "Course Suggestion" as your subject.

Are you open to partnerships?

Yes, we welcome collaborations with educational institutions, NGOs, and development organizations. Please email us at partnerships@impactmojo.in with your proposal.

Can I contribute content to ImpactMojo?

We're always looking for subject matter experts to contribute. Please contact us with your expertise area and a brief proposal.
